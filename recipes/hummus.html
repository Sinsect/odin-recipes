Hummus

Description

A simple recipe for tasty hummus. A food processor is preferred but not required. I personally use an immersion blender, which occasionally leaves me with some chunks but I'm okay with that.

Modifications can be made to suit taste.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon
  • Cumin
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Whip up the lemon juice and tahini in the blender.
  2. Add everything else except the chickpeas and paprika and blend.
  3. Separate chickpeas from chickpea-water liquid (aquafaba).
  4. Slowly add chickpeas while blending.
  5. If too thick, slowly add aquafaba or water til ideal consistency is reached.
  6. Serve drizzled with olive oil and paprika.
