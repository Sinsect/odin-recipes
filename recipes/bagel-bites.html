Bagel Bites

Description

A quick snack for tired times.

Air fryer instructions provided, but you could figure something out for other cooking methods.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Bagel bites

Steps

  1. Place bagel bites on a single layer in air fryer, spacing evenly
  2. Cook at 400F for around 5 minutes, checking every so often for doneness.
  3. Enjoy!
